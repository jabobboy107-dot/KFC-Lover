I Love Thee KFC

O golden crispy delight, my heart's own plea,
In every tender bite, I find sweet ecstasy.
Thy seasoned warmth ignites a passionate fire,
A love more rich than any soul's desire.

Each drumstick whispers tales of zest and grace,
A savory sonnet time cannot erase.
In fragrant clouds of spice, our souls entwine,
oh KFC, forever shalt thou be mine.
