Letter To Colonel Sanders

Dear Sanders, I love you. You are my joy, my delight. You fill my heart with peace.
The thought of you makes me melt. Your exquisite chicken, your delicious selection of spices, it all makes me cry.
Colonel Sanders, You fill me with happiness. I miss you more than ever right now.
I long for just one bite of your delicious, juicy, finger-lickin good chicken. I love you Sanders.
From: Jacob
